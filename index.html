about us

Toyiyo is an easy-to-use online project management tool for small businesses, teams, and individuals. It's perfect for staying on top of the milestones and day-to-day tasks involved with your work.

  • Join with ease, we support self registration via social auth like google as well as user name and password.
  • Working on multiple projects? Manage unlimited projects with ease.
  • Focus on what matters with our ability to filter your jobs by status.
  • Organize it your way! Our drag-and-drop interface lets you keep your jobs in the order that matters to you.
  • Stay on top of your work with our ability to set due dates for your jobs.

We are a small team of developers who are passionate about building great products. We are just getting started and we are excited to see where this journey takes us.
